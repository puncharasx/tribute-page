Freddie Mercury

“I won’t be a rock star. I will be a legend.”

Timeline

  • 1946 Freddie Mercury was born in Zanzibar, Tanzania on September 5
  • 1954 At the Age of eight, Freddie began his education at St. Peters English boarding school (near Bombay, India)
  • 1964 Freddie Mercury moved to Middlesex, England in 1964, at the age of 17.
  • 1967 Freddie Mercury joined a band called Smile.
  • 1970 Freddie Mercury took over as the lead singer of Smile, and renamed the band Queen.
  • 1975 The album "A Night At The Opera" was released, and The song Bohemian Rhapsody was released on the album entitled "A Night at the Opera".
  • 1991 The day before he died, Freddie Mercury announced that he had AIDS, and He died of bronchial pneumonia on November 24, 1991.
